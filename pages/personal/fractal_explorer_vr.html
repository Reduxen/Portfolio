Fractal Explorer VR

Fractal Explorer VR lets you easily visualize, manipulate and navaigate various 3D fractals in a VR space. You can choose from 9 unique fractals formulas, such as the julia-set or the mandelbrot-set. There are also 6 presets which put a spin on the fractal, like different colors or parameters. 4 additional animations bring some life into the otherwise static fractals. Just lean back and enjoy, as the Fractals morph and move.

With Fractal Explorer VR I wanted to give as much control over the Fractals as possible, while also not having overcomplicated menus. To acheive this I created a 3D menu seperated into 3 sections: General, Fractal and Color. This lets the menu be less cluttered. Only one of these sections is presented to the user at a time and the active section can be switched by rotating the menu around your hand.

One special feature in the fractal menu is that you can select parameters by clicking at them with your laser pointer and then change them with your controller's joystick. This makes fine tuning and smooth transitions possible. The selected parameter is highligted in blue.
