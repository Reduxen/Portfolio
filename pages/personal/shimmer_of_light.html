In Shimmer of Light you play as a character named Photon, who has one very special ability: they can change the time of day in an instant. The time of day influences the world around you significantly. Shadows become solid, monsters wake up and platforms start moving. This makes for some quick reaction time platforming with a high skill ceiling, aswell as some puzzles to solve along the way.

With this project I wanted to make a difficult, but responsive sidescroller platformer, with the special ability to bring background elements into the foreground and vise versa. To achieve this I used Unity's 2D framework, which provides a collision system and a grid system to create the levels. The rest like the character movement and camera were custom scripted, since movement is one of the key aspects of the game.
